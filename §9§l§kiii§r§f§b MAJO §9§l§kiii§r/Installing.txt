- Installation instructions (Windows) -
- 1 - Go to the Windows Start menu and type: %AppData% and open the folder called AppData.
- 2 - Inside AppData, go to Roaming>.minecraft>Resourcepacks.
- 3 - Drag this folder onto that folder(the entire file, as you downloaded it)
- 4 - You should now be able to use it in Minecraft!
